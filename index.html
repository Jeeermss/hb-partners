HB Partners is a boutique provider in private placement and equity/debt capital services for upper-small to middle-market companies lead by Aleathia Hoster and Tim Brown, with securities offered through Sunbelt Securities, Inc., member of FINRA and SIPC.

HB's commitment is to consistently provide operating companies with high quality, strategies advisory and capital raising services for alternative asset classes, with a primary focus on real estate and distressed debt.

Core to HB's business values are the practiced discipline of identifying and engaing the most suitable investors, efficiently marketing the investment opportunity and clearly communicating the investment opportunity with strategic investors, private equity firms and fmaily offices (with over 14 combined years of family office experience).
